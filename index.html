Approach

  1. Clarity before decoration

    Structure, hierarchy, and language come first. Visual refinement should make meaning easier to feel, not harder to find.

  2. Restraint as confidence

    Strong work does not need to raise its voice. The goal is a site that feels composed, precise, and considered.

  3. AI with a real job to do

    I use AI where it improves utility, speed, or understanding. I avoid adding it where it only adds novelty.
